About Us

we are
Wembley Interior

professional interior Agency

We make your building more perfect with 32 years of experience

We at Wembley Interior (P) Ltd are passionate about designing and creating spaces which make your living and working life simplified. We create fusion between creativity and professional expertise with latest technology to give you a sense of comfort and prestige.

We have devoted our energy and enthusiasm in designing spaces starting from visualization to its materialization till our client’s satisfaction successfully. We believe and emphasize on the detailing and finishing of fabrication which is most essential part in the field of interior works. Its all about great design and finishing of the product. A well-designed space physically and psychologically enhances the performance to a great extent in our lives.

why choose us

fast building

Our all-inclusive interior packages conceived by experienced architects designed to complete in a short time frame to avoid delay in possession and early moving into your dream home.
 

Optimised Design

Our contemporary style in Interior designing and furniture inspired by Italian Look reflects a refined stylish ambience. Every Home will be designed to be distinctly different from all others. Choose your favourite design, Colour, Texture from our Design Library.

save money

We are a vertically Integrated Interior Company providing end to end solutions. We are National Small Scale Industries Corporation (NSIC) registered unit in furniture manufacturing and Interior decoration works receiving discount in raw materials.

Quality

Quality of materials is absolutely paramount for you and us. We strictly use High quality Plywood and Block boards confirming to BIS standards for all furniture & EU Standard Hardware making it Strong, Majestic and durable through generation for customer delight.
